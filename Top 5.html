My Top 5 Heavy Metal bands

My top ten favourite metal bands is probably the hardest list I have to think of as there are so much bands I love and it is a list that is constantly fluctuating. So as it stands right now, here are my top 10 heavy metal bands.

Machine are a four piece heavy metal band from California. They consist of Robb Flynn (singer), Dave McClain (Drummer), Phil Demmel (Guitarist) and Jared McEachern (Bassist). Heavy and groovy, these are four guys that love the music they create and the thrill of the live tour. Recommended tracks: Imperium, Davidium, Old, Unto the Locust and The Blackening

Pantera are a four pice metal band from deep within Texas. Pantera are sadly no longer a band due the passing of guitarist "Dimebag" Darrell Abbott. Other Members included Drummer Vinnie Abbott, Bassist Rex Brown and lead singer Phil Anselmo. Recommended tracks: Walk, Cowboys From Hell, Mouth For War, I'm Broken and Cemetery Gates

The most well-known band on this list, Metallica started out in San Fransisco in the 1980's when the Thrash metal scene of Northern California was at it's booming point. Yet another four piece band of James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Kirk Hammett and nopw onto third bassist Robert Trujillo. Previous bassists were Cliff Burton and Jason Newstead. Recommeded tracks: Master Of Puppets, Fade To Black, Enter Sandman, Sad But True and ...And Justice For All

Recommended tracks: Before I Forget, Duality, Wait and Bleed, Custer and Negative One

My Favourite Band of all time, Lamb Of God is a five piece metal band from Richmond, Virginia. Lamb Of God are a heavy and aggressive band who are outspoken and proud of it, songs occasionally poking fun of politics and religion. Recommended Tracks: Contractor,Laid To Rest, Redneck, The Undertow and Ruin.
